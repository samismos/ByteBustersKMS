The Binary Bible

We believe that everyone should be able to understand their own devices. Because of this, our goal is to provide a tutorial that is simple enough for novices and less tech-savvy individuals to follow without the use of unnecessary technical jargon. The Binary Bible will help you understand the basic concept of how a computer works, what each component does and what you should look out for. After reading our BB, you will be an informed buyer ready to enter the PC hardware market with a bang!
